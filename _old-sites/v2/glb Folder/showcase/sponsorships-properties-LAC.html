Sponsorships

The Sponsorship section provides an overview of our Global Sponsorship Strategy along with a summary of our Sponsorship properties. Additionally, this section offers a behind the scenes look at sponsorship and marketing teams on the ground and our Team Visa athletes.

  • Strategy
  • Top 5

Through a variety of assets, our 50+ sport and entertainment sponsorships around the world effectively amplify our marketing and enable signifcant merchant activation.

If you would like more information on a specific sponsorship, please contact the property manager listed below the sponsorship description.

LAC

Copa do Brasil – Copa do Brasil is one of the two national club level football leagues in Brazil. With football being a passion experienced daily in Brazil, it was important for Visa to be a part of this important local tournament which is leveraged as part of Visa's football platforms.

Contact: Yuri Morales

Copa Bridgestone Libertadores – Since 2004, Visa has been a main sponsor of Copa Bridgestone Libertadores, an international soccer tournament that includes participation of 38 teams from ten South American countries and Mexico. The sponsorship is another example of Visa's commitment to the world's most loved sport.

Contact: Yuri Morales

FEMEXFUT/Mexican National Team – Visa's global football platform is brought to life in Mexico through its sponsorship of the Football Mexican Federation. From beach to futsal to the team that competes to qualify in the FIFA World Cup Brazil 2014, all football Mexican National teams are part of Visa's sponsorship with access to each teams IP, promotional rights and tickets to all locally played matches.

Contact: Yuri Morales

Puerto Rico & Panama Entertainment – Visa has partnered with the main event promoters in Panama and Puerto Rico to provide Visa and its Clients in each markets with marketing assets, exclusive access to top events and promotional opportunities around touring events such as Cirque du Soleil and Lady Gaga among others.

Contact: Yuri Morales
