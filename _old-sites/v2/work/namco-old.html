Bandai Namco

Every good story starts with a name. But when Bandai Namco came to us with this challenge, their game didn’t even have that. So we jumped head first into the backstory and made it our mission to know what their developers were thinking. After hours of picking their brains—and coming up with nearly 100 name options—we landed on a winner: Rise of Incarnates.

Next we hit the design, coming up with a variety of type and mark treatments that felt authentic to playing the game. Then we went to work hyping the game launch with a fully responsive website. And we followed that winning act with a second, more robust site, once the game was beta-ready.

Happy gamers, happy clients.

We created custom typography with a sci-fi/military feel.
It worked well with the chaos and destruction found in the game and the settings.

Responsive X2

Over the course of this game launch, we designed two fully responsive sites: The first—a teaser experience to get players excited about the upcoming release. The second—a richer site where users could explore and engage with all aspects of the game.

From typeface
to interface

After developing a name and design language for the game identity and marketing sites, we worked with the development team to create the in-game UI. This ensured to a consistent look and feel across all interactions with the brand.

But what’s more challenging to find in an agency partner is the strategy and execution of a good idea or creative concept. ANML can do both. And in my experience, that’s rare and exciting."
Ryan GrissomSr. Manager Digital Platform | Bandai Namco
